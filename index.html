About Us

Telecall Institure of Technology Inc.

Telecall Laguna is your trusted gateway to a world of opportunities in Technical and Vocational Education (TVET). We proudly hold accreditation from both the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) and the Commission on Higher Education (CHED), ensuring that our training courses are of the highest quality and relevance. At Telecall Laguna, we are committed to empowering individuals with the practical skills and knowledge needed to excel in today's competitive job market. Our institution provides a nurturing learning environment, led by experienced instructors, and equipped with state-of-the-art facilities. Whether you are looking to kickstart a new career or enhance your existing skills, Telecall Laguna is your partner in achieving your educational and career aspirations. Join us on a journey to success and personal growth.

Mission

"Telecall Institute of Laguna is committed to delivering an outstanding education that empowers our students to attain academic excellence, personal development, and professional achievements."

Vision

"At Telecall Institute of Laguna, we aspire to create a future where each student unlocks their full potential, becoming a shining symbol of knowledge and a force for positive change. Our primary objective is to nurture, educate, and inspire students, guiding them towards personal growth and leadership, with the ultimate goal of contributing positively to society."

    Who is eligible to apply to Telecall Institute of Technology?

    To apply to Telecall Institute of Technology, applicants must meet the following requirements:

  • Be at least 18 years old.
  • Possess a valid ID photocopy.
  • Provide the necessary documents, including:
  • 2 copies of the Birth Certificate
  • 2 copies of the Marriage Certificate (if married)
  • 2 copies of the Diploma
  • 2 copies of Form 138/138/TOR/ALS Certificate
  • 3 pieces of 1x1-inch photos
  • 2 pieces of 2x2-inch photos
  • 2 pieces of passport-sized photos

    • How do I apply to Telecall Institute of Technology?

    To apply, follow these steps:

  • Complete the online application form on our website.
  • Prepare all required documents, including the ones mentioned in Question 1.
  • Submit your application and documents to our admissions office in person or through our designated application portal.

    • What programs does Telecall Institute of Technology offer?

    Telecall Institute of Technology offers a variety of academic programs. Please visit our Programs page on our website for a detailed list of available courses and programs.

    What is the application deadline?

    Application deadlines may vary depending on the program and semester. Please refer to our Admissions page on the website or contact our admissions office for specific application deadlines.

    How long does the application review process take?

    The application review process typically takes several weeks. You will be notified of your application status via email or mail once the review is complete.
